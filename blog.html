About Me

I am a Siberian Forest Cat living in Victoria, Australia, and my breath smells like cat food. My owners named me Lulu, with the youngest giving me the middle names Cupcake Sparkle Fart. My likes include sitting in laundry buckets, staring longingly out of the window at birds, and shedding my long fur everywhere. My dislikes include yoga mats and my harness.
