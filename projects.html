Pilot Logbook

December 2021

My first ever full-stack application, created in just a few short weeks. An application for pilots to log their flight hours, this necessitated a rather extensive PostgreSQL database, SQLAlchemy ORM to protect against SQL injection attacks and Flask-Login to implement a secure authorisation system. A work in progress, owing in no small part to the sheer number of fields in the database, my next step is to host this on the cloud.
