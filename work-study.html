Real Estate Advertising Coordinator

Geelong, Victoria

September 2004 to July 2006

In this role I created real estate advertisements for three publications, all while adhering to strict styles guides.

While the accounts department had a database of customers, we in the advertising department did not. Hand edited letters to customers were time consuming to create and prone to errors, and the company gave us few software resources (we even had to share one computer among five of us - the dark ages!) So I created a database using Microsoft Access and marketing documents using MailMerge in Word. This was all self taught by trial and error - I did not have ready access to tutorials. As a result of my efforts, communications to dozens of customers took minutes instead of days, and my only regret is not calling it Salesforce and making billions of dollars.
